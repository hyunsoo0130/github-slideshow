description: "Create a step-by-step tutorial card news series in 8 slides. Perfect for Instagram/LinkedIn educational carousels — covers hook, steps, tips, and CTA in a clear visual flow."
tool: "claude"
tags: ["tutorial", "how-to", "card news", "instagram", "educational", "carousel"]
date: "2026-04-02"
promptText: |
  Create an 8-slide how-to tutorial card news series: "How to [TUTORIAL TOPIC]"
  Target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Platform: Instagram/LinkedIn carousel.

  SLIDE 1 — HOOK
  Headline: "How to [TUTORIAL TOPIC] in [X] easy steps"
  Sub: "Even if you've never done it before"
  Visual direction: Bold color, contrasting text, curiosity-driven

  SLIDE 2 — WHY THIS MATTERS
  "Most people [COMMON MISTAKE] — here's what to do instead."
  Identify the pain + promise of the solution (2–3 lines)

  SLIDES 3–6 — STEPS 1 THROUGH 4
  Each slide = one step:
  Large step number (01/02/03/04) | Step name | 2–3 clear instructions | Pro tip in italic

  SLIDE 7 — COMMON PITFALLS
  "Avoid these mistakes:" — 3 mistakes people make with quick fixes

  SLIDE 8 — CALL TO ACTION
  "Save this for later 🔖" + "Follow for more [TOPIC] tips"
  Your handle or website

  Write all copy for [TUTORIAL TOPIC] — make it clear, actionable, and encouraging.
usage: |
  Paste into Claude with your tutorial topic. Claude writes all 8 slides of copy.
  Then design in Canva or Figma using your brand template.
variables: |
  [TUTORIAL TOPIC] → What you're teaching. Examples: "negotiate a salary raise",
                     "set up Google Analytics 4", "make sourdough bread", "cold email for freelancers"
  [TARGET AUDIENCE] → Who will read it. Affects tone and complexity.
  [X] → Number of steps (3–5 is ideal for carousels)
tips: |
  • "Save this post" carousels get 3× more shares — always include a save prompt
  • Keep each step slide to 30 words max for mobile readability
  • Ask Claude to write 3 alternative hook headlines and pick the strongest
