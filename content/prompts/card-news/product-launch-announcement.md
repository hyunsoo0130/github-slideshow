title: "Product Launch Announcement Card News"
description: "Create a 7-slide product launch announcement carousel for social media. Covers teaser, problem, product reveal, key features, social proof, pricing, and CTA — designed to generate pre-launch hype."
tool: "claude"
tags: ["product launch", "announcement", "card news", "instagram", "marketing", "hype"]
date: "2026-04-03"
promptText: |
  Create a 7-slide product launch announcement card news for [PRODUCT NAME] by [BRAND NAME].
  Launch date: [LAUNCH DATE]. Price: [PRICE]. Platform: Instagram/LinkedIn carousel.

  SLIDE 1 — TEASER
  "Something big is coming." + [LAUNCH DATE] in large text
  Visual: Dark background, minimal, mysterious. Brand color accent only.
  No product name yet — build anticipation.

  SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM (your audience's pain)
  "You know that feeling when [RELATABLE PROBLEM SCENARIO]?"
  3 pain points as quick bullets. End with: "We've been there. That's why we built [PRODUCT NAME]."

  SLIDE 3 — THE REVEAL
  "[PRODUCT NAME] is here." — bold, centered, triumphant
  One-sentence product description. Hero image placeholder (product shot).
  Brand color explosion — most visually bold slide in the deck.

  SLIDE 4 — KEY FEATURES (3 features)
  "What makes [PRODUCT NAME] different:"
  Feature 1: [FEATURE NAME] — [BENEFIT in 1 sentence]
  Feature 2: [FEATURE NAME] — [BENEFIT in 1 sentence]
  Feature 3: [FEATURE NAME] — [BENEFIT in 1 sentence]
  Use icons. Clean, confident layout.

  SLIDE 5 — SOCIAL PROOF
  "Don't just take our word for it." — Beta tester or early user quote
  "[TESTIMONIAL]" — [NAME], [ROLE/COMPANY]
  OR: Early access stats: "[X] people on the waitlist. [X] beta testers. [X]% satisfaction."

  SLIDE 6 — PRICING & OFFER
  Introductory launch price: [PRICE] (regular: [REGULAR PRICE])
  Launch offer: "First [X] customers get [DISCOUNT/BONUS]"
  Countdown: "Offer ends [DATE]" — creates urgency

  SLIDE 7 — CALL TO ACTION
  "Get [PRODUCT NAME] now →"
  Link in bio / direct link / QR code placeholder
  "Questions? DM us or email [EMAIL]"
  Social proof closer: "[X] people already joined today"

  Write compelling launch copy for [PRODUCT NAME] targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE].
usage: |
  Post all 7 slides as a single carousel 24–48 hours after teasing on stories.
  Pin the launch carousel to your profile during launch week.
variables: |
  [PRODUCT NAME] → Name of the product/service launching
  [BRAND NAME] → Your brand/company
  [LAUNCH DATE] → When it goes live
  [PRICE] / [REGULAR PRICE] → Introductory vs regular pricing
  [TARGET AUDIENCE] → Who this is for
tips: |
  • Post Slide 1 as a story teaser 3–5 days before posting the full carousel
  • Add a "Swipe to see everything →" CTA on Slide 1 to increase carousel completion rate
  • Request "write 5 alternative headlines for Slide 3" and A/B test across platforms
