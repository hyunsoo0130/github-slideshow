  Create a 10-slide industry insights card news series on "[INDUSTRY/TOPIC] in [YEAR]" for LinkedIn.
  Brand: [BRAND/AUTHOR NAME]. Target reader: [TARGET AUDIENCE].
  This is a thought leadership carousel — data-rich, authoritative, visually bold.

  SLIDE 1 — HOOK STAT
  "[SHOCKING STATISTIC about INDUSTRY/TOPIC]"
  Source: [SOURCE NAME]
  Sub: "Here's what it means for [TARGET AUDIENCE] →"
  Design: Dark background, large white number, minimal.

  SLIDE 2 — CONTEXT
  "The [INDUSTRY] landscape in [YEAR]:"
  3 key context points that set up the rest of the report.
  Small chart/graph placeholder showing trend direction.

  SLIDE 3 — TREND #1
  "Trend 1: [TREND NAME]"
  What's happening: 2 sentences
  Why it matters: 1 sentence
  Stat: "[X]% increase/decrease in [METRIC]" — [SOURCE]
  Visual: Icon + stat callout

  SLIDE 4 — TREND #2
  Same format as Slide 3 but for [TREND 2]

  SLIDE 5 — TREND #3
  Same format for [TREND 3]

  SLIDE 6 — THE OPPORTUNITY
  "Here's the opportunity most [TARGET AUDIENCE] are missing:"
  Clear gap in the market + how to position for it.
  Actionable framing: "Companies that [ACTION] will [BENEFIT]."

  SLIDE 7 — EXPERT PERSPECTIVE
  "[EXPERT QUOTE about the trend]" — [Expert Name, Title, Company]
  OR: "Our take:" — [YOUR 2-3 sentence original analysis]
  Design: Quote card, clean, authoritative typography.

  SLIDE 8 — DATA VISUALIZATION
  "[KEY DATA POINT]" presented as a simple visual:
  Bar chart / Pie chart / Before-After comparison / Side-by-side stat blocks
  Caption: Source, date, methodology note.

  SLIDE 9 — WHAT TO DO ABOUT IT
  "3 actions [TARGET AUDIENCE] should take in [YEAR]:"
  Action 1: [SPECIFIC TACTIC] → Expected result
  Action 2: [SPECIFIC TACTIC] → Expected result
  Action 3: [SPECIFIC TACTIC] → Expected result

  SLIDE 10 — CLOSE & CTA
  "The [YEAR] [INDUSTRY] report — key takeaway:"
  1-paragraph summary of the most important insight.
  "Follow [BRAND/AUTHOR NAME] for weekly [INDUSTRY] insights."
  "Save this for your next team discussion 🔖"

  Generate complete content with realistic statistics and insights on [INDUSTRY/TOPIC].
usage: |
  Post as LinkedIn carousel on Monday or Tuesday morning for maximum reach.
  Tag the expert quoted (Slide 7) for additional organic amplification.
  Repurpose: slides 3–5 can each be standalone posts.

  [INDUSTRY/TOPIC] → e.g., "AI in healthcare", "creator economy", "B2B SaaS pricing", "remote work"
