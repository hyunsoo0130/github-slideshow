  HEADER SECTION

  1. [ITEM TITLE] — 2–3 sentence explanation with source link placeholder
  2. [ITEM TITLE] — same format
  3. [ITEM TITLE] — same format

  SECTION 3: TOOL / RESOURCE OF THE WEEK
  Name: "[TOOL NAME]"
  What it does: 2 sentences
  Who it's for: 1 sentence
  Link: [URL placeholder]
  Rating: ★★★★☆ (with brief justification)

  SECTION 4: QUOTE OF THE WEEK
  "[INSPIRING OR RELEVANT QUOTE]" — [ATTRIBUTION]

  SECTION 5: FROM THE COMMUNITY (optional)
  Reader question + author answer (3–5 lines each)
  OR reader spotlight/submission

  FOOTER
  Unsubscribe link | Privacy Policy | View in browser
  "You're receiving this because you subscribed at [WEBSITE]"
  Social media icons row: Twitter/X | LinkedIn | Instagram
  Copyright notice

  Generate complete newsletter content on the topic of [MAIN TOPIC].

  1. Fill in your newsletter name, audience, and topic

  5. Send!
