promptText: |
  Create a freelance service contract between [FREELANCER NAME] ("Service Provider") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") for [SERVICE TYPE] services.

  CONTRACT SECTIONS:

  1. PARTIES & EFFECTIVE DATE
  Full names, business names (if applicable), addresses, and contract date.

  2. SCOPE OF WORK
  Deliverables: [LIST SPECIFIC DELIVERABLES]
  Timeline: Project start [DATE] | Final delivery by [DATE]
  Revision rounds included: [NUMBER] rounds of revisions
  Out-of-scope clause: "Any work beyond the agreed scope requires a separate written agreement and additional fee."

  3. COMPENSATION
  Total project fee: [AMOUNT] [CURRENCY]
  Payment schedule:
  - [X]% ([AMOUNT]) due upon signing (deposit)
  - [X]% ([AMOUNT]) due upon [MILESTONE]
  - [X]% ([AMOUNT]) due upon final delivery
  Late payment: Interest of [X]% per month on overdue amounts after [X] days.
  Invoices sent via: [METHOD]

  4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
  Upon receipt of full payment, all deliverables become sole property of [CLIENT NAME].
  Service Provider retains the right to display the work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed.
  Pre-existing tools, templates, and frameworks used remain property of Service Provider.

  5. CONFIDENTIALITY
  Both parties agree to keep all project details, business information, and communications confidential for [X] years.
  Exception: information already in public domain.

  6. REVISIONS & APPROVAL
  Client has [X] business days to request revisions after each deliverable.
  Silence after [X] days = implicit approval.
  Each additional revision round beyond [NUMBER] included: [AMOUNT] per round.

  7. TERMINATION
  Either party may terminate with [X] days written notice.
  Client pays for all work completed to termination date.
  Deposit is non-refundable.

  8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
  Service Provider's liability limited to total amount paid under this contract.
  No liability for indirect or consequential damages.

  9. GOVERNING LAW
  This contract governed by the laws of [JURISDICTION].
  Disputes resolved by [ARBITRATION / MEDIATION / LOCAL COURTS].

  10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
  This contract supersedes all prior agreements. Amendments require written consent.

  SIGNATURE BLOCKS
  Service Provider: _____________ Date: _____________
  Client: _____________ Date: _____________

  Generate the complete contract with professional legal-style language but plain English throughout.
usage: |
  This is a template — not legal advice. For high-value contracts, have a lawyer review.
  Fill in all variables and paste into Claude for the complete contract text.
  Copy the output into Word, add your letterhead, and send as PDF.
variables: |
  [FREELANCER NAME] / [CLIENT NAME] → Full legal names
  [SERVICE TYPE] → "web design" / "copywriting" / "video editing" / "software development"
  [LIST SPECIFIC DELIVERABLES] → Be very specific — vague scope = disputes
  [AMOUNT] / [CURRENCY] → Pricing and currency
  [JURISDICTION] → Your country/state for governing law
tips: |
  • The more specific the scope of work, the fewer disputes you'll have
  • Ask Claude to "add a kill fee clause of 25% if client cancels after work begins"
  • Request "add a non-solicitation clause preventing client from hiring my subcontractors"
  • For ongoing retainers: ask Claude to "convert this to a monthly retainer agreement"
