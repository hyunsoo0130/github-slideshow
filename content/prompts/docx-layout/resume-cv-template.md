---
title: "Professional Resume / CV Layout"
description: "Create a clean, ATS-friendly resume layout in Word with contact header, summary, experience, education, and skills sections. Includes formatting tips to pass applicant tracking systems."
tool: "claude"
tags: ["resume", "cv", "word", "job", "career", "ats"]
date: "2026-04-04"
promptText: |
  Create a professional resume layout in Microsoft Word for [YOUR NAME] applying for [TARGET ROLE] positions in [INDUSTRY].

  LAYOUT: Single column, clean, ATS-optimized. No tables, text boxes, or graphics that confuse ATS.
  Font: Calibri 11pt body. Name: 24pt bold. Section headers: 13pt bold with bottom border rule.
  Margins: 0.75 inch all sides. Page limit: 1–2 pages.

  SECTION 1: CONTACT HEADER (top, centered or left-aligned)
  [FULL NAME] — 24pt bold
  [CITY, STATE] | [PHONE] | [EMAIL] | [LINKEDIN URL] | [PORTFOLIO URL if applicable]

  SECTION 2: PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY (3–4 lines)
  "[X] years of experience in [FIELD]. Specialized in [SPECIALIZATION].
  Known for [KEY STRENGTH 1] and [KEY STRENGTH 2]. Seeking to [CAREER GOAL] at [COMPANY TYPE]."
  Write this in third-person-free, confident first-person voice.

  SECTION 3: WORK EXPERIENCE
  For each role (most recent first):
  [JOB TITLE] | [COMPANY NAME] | [CITY] | [START DATE] – [END DATE or Present]
  • Achievement bullet 1: [ACTION VERB] + [WHAT YOU DID] + [MEASURABLE RESULT]
  • Achievement bullet 2: same formula
  • Achievement bullet 3: same formula
  (3–5 bullets per role, starting with strongest)
  Roles to include: [LIST YOUR ROLES]

  SECTION 4: EDUCATION
  [DEGREE] in [MAJOR] | [UNIVERSITY NAME] | [GRADUATION YEAR]
  GPA: [X.X]/4.0 (only if 3.5+) | Relevant coursework: [2–3 courses if entry-level]
  Honors/Awards: [IF ANY]

  SECTION 5: SKILLS
  Technical Skills: [COMMA-SEPARATED LIST]
  Tools & Software: [COMMA-SEPARATED LIST]
  Languages: [IF APPLICABLE]
  Certifications: [CERT NAME] — [ISSUER] — [YEAR]

  SECTION 6: OPTIONAL SECTIONS (add if relevant)
  Projects | Volunteer Work | Publications | Speaking | Awards

  ATS OPTIMIZATION RULES (apply throughout):
  • Use exact keywords from [TARGET ROLE] job descriptions
  • Spell out acronyms once: "Search Engine Optimization (SEO)"
  • No images, logos, headers/footers (ATS often can't read them)
  • Standard section names: "Work Experience" not "My Journey"

  Generate a complete resume for a [EXPERIENCE LEVEL] [TARGET ROLE] with realistic placeholder data.
usage: |
  1. Fill in your actual information for all variables
  2. Paste into Claude — it generates your full resume text
  3. Copy into a blank Word document
  4. Apply the font and spacing rules described
  5. Save as .docx AND .pdf (some ATS prefer PDF)

  Pro tip: Ask Claude to tailor your resume for a specific job posting by pasting the job description.
variables: |
  [YOUR NAME] → Full legal name as you want it on the resume
  [TARGET ROLE] → The job title you're applying for
  [INDUSTRY] → Your field (e.g., "technology", "healthcare", "finance")
  [EXPERIENCE LEVEL] → "entry-level" / "mid-level (5 years)" / "senior (10+ years)"
  [FIELD] → Your professional field
  [SPECIALIZATION] → Your niche expertise
  [KEY STRENGTH 1-2] → Top professional qualities
  [LIST YOUR ROLES] → Up to 4 previous positions
tips: |
  • Ask Claude: "Rewrite my bullets using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)"
  • Add "Tailor this resume for this specific job posting: [paste job description]"
  • Request "identify gaps between my resume and this job description"
  • For career changers: ask "reframe my [OLD INDUSTRY] experience for [NEW INDUSTRY]"
  • Numbers always beat vague claims — ask Claude to "add estimated metrics to each bullet"
