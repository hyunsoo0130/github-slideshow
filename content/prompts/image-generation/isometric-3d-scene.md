---
title: "Isometric 3D Scene"
description: "Generate a clean isometric 3D illustration of a workspace, city block, room, or conceptual scene. Perfect for explainer graphics, app illustrations, and infographic headers."
tool: "nano-banana"
tags: ["isometric", "3d", "illustration", "infographic", "scene", "flat"]
date: "2026-04-06"
promptText: |
  Create a clean isometric 3D illustration of [SCENE DESCRIPTION].

  Isometric projection: True 30° isometric view. All vertical lines remain perfectly
  vertical. All horizontal edges go to two vanishing points at 30° from horizontal.
  Consistent isometric grid throughout — no perspective distortion.

  Style: Clean, minimal 3D illustration. Think: Behance isometric design, Notion-style
  illustrations, or Google Material Design 3D. Smooth surfaces, no texture details.
  Shadows: flat, simple, single-direction (lower-right). No ambient occlusion complexity.

  Scene: [SCENE DESCRIPTION].
  Key elements: [LIST 3-5 SPECIFIC OBJECTS IN THE SCENE].

  Color palette: [COLOR PALETTE DESCRIPTION].
  All objects use flat colors from this palette. No photorealistic materials.
  Background: flat white or very light [ACCENT COLOR]. Clean.

  Level of detail: Medium — enough detail to be interesting, not so much it becomes
  cluttered. Each object recognizable but stylized.

  Size: Composition fits within a square. Centered with breathing room on all sides.
  Aspect ratio: 1:1.

  Output feel: SaaS website hero illustration. App store feature graphic.
  Notion template cover. Friendly, professional, modern.
usage: |
  1. Open Nano Banana
  2. Describe your scene clearly — what environment, what objects
  3. List 3–5 specific objects you want included
  4. Choose a 3–4 color palette (recommend analogous colors for harmony)
  5. Generate and tweak by being more specific about object placement

  Great for: website hero sections, app illustrations, infographic headers,
  presentation covers, explainer video stills, and icon sets
variables: |
  [SCENE DESCRIPTION] → The environment/setting
    Examples:
    - "a home office workspace with monitor, plant, and coffee cup"
    - "a small coffee shop with counter, espresso machine, and two café tables"
    - "a city park with trees, bench, fountain, and walking path"
    - "a hospital reception area with front desk and waiting chairs"

  [LIST 3-5 SPECIFIC OBJECTS] → Key items in the scene
    Examples: "standing desk, dual monitors, mechanical keyboard, desk lamp, small succulent"

  [COLOR PALETTE DESCRIPTION] → 3–4 colors
    Examples:
    - "indigo blue, soft white, warm beige, light gray"
    - "coral orange, cream, sage green, light brown"
    - "deep navy, sky blue, white, gold accent"
    - Brand colors: "brand primary #6366F1, light #E0E7FF, white, dark gray"
tips: |
  • Limit your scene to 5–8 objects for the cleanest result
  • Isometric works best for contained environments — rooms, desks, storefronts
  • Add "tiny human figures for scale" if you want characters in the scene
  • For a more cartoon feel: add "rounded corners on all objects, friendly proportions"
  • For tech company style: add "floating UI windows and data visualizations above the scene"
  • Request "exploded view, all components separated and floating with gap between them" for a technical diagram feel
