---
title: "Minimal Product Mockup"
description: "Generate a clean, studio-quality product mockup on a pure white or minimal background. Suitable for e-commerce, branding presentations, and social media product posts."
tool: "nano-banana"
tags: ["product", "mockup", "studio", "ecommerce", "minimal", "white"]
date: "2026-04-05"
promptText: |
  Professional product studio photograph of [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION].

  Product: [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION]. Color: [PRODUCT COLOR].
  Condition: Brand new, pristine, no marks or fingerprints.
  Label/branding: [LABEL TEXT OR NONE].

  Photography style: High-end commercial product photography.
  Clean, minimal, premium retail catalog aesthetic.

  Surface: The product sits on a pure white (#FFFFFF) infinity background surface.
  There is a very subtle, soft reflection of the product on the surface below it —
  not a mirror reflection, just a faint echo (opacity ~15%).
  No harsh shadows. One soft diffused shadow directly beneath, spread radius 40px.

  Lighting: Large soft box from upper front-left, 5500K daylight temperature.
  Fill card from right (70% power). Top strip light for highlight separation.
  Result: even, professional, shadowless impression. Product pops cleanly from white.

  Camera: Eye-level or 10–15° above for most products.
  OR: 30° elevated for flat/top-view products (specify).
  Depth of field: sharp throughout — every detail in focus.
  Aspect ratio: 1:1 for social media. 4:3 for e-commerce. Specify preference.

  Output feel: Apple product launch. MUJI catalog. High-end D2C brand.
  White space, precision, restraint.
usage: |
  1. Open Nano Banana
  2. Replace [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION] with your item
  3. Specify the color and any branding/label details
  4. Choose your preferred background (pure white, soft gradient, or colored)
  5. Generate multiple variations and pick the most photogenic composition

  Ideal for: e-commerce listings, Shopify product images, Instagram shop posts,
  pitch deck product slides, and brand portfolio mockups
variables: |
  [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION] → The item to photograph. Be specific:
    Good: "matte black stainless steel water bottle, cylindrical, 500ml, screw-top lid"
    Good: "minimalist unboxed smartphone, screen off, centered"
    Too vague: "a bottle" / "a phone"

  [PRODUCT COLOR] → Color(s) of the product
    Examples: "matte midnight black" / "warm ivory white" / "sage green" / "brushed gold"

  [LABEL TEXT OR NONE] → Brand name/text on the product, or write "no visible text"
    Examples: "BRAND NAME in clean sans-serif on front face" / "no branding, clean surface"

  Background options (add to prompt):
    - "pure white infinity background" (default, e-commerce)
    - "light concrete texture surface, white wall behind" (lifestyle/editorial)
    - "pastel [COLOR] soft gradient background" (social media/Instagram)
tips: |
  • The more specific the product description, the more accurate the result
  • Add "packaging box open beside product" for unboxing style shots
  • For fashion/apparel: add "on a clean ghost mannequin, flat lighting"
  • Add "golden hour side lighting" to shift from studio to lifestyle mood
  • For food products: add "dewy condensation drops on surface" for freshness
  • Request "3/4 angle view, 30° rotation showing product profile" for dynamic composition
