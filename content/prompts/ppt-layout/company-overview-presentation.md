tool: "claude"
tags: ["company", "overview", "presentation", "b2b", "sales", "ppt"]
date: "2026-04-03"
promptText: |
  Create an 10-slide company overview presentation for [COMPANY NAME].
  Industry: [INDUSTRY]. Founded: [YEAR]. Size: [COMPANY SIZE].

  SLIDE 1: COVER — Company logo area, company name, tagline: "[TAGLINE]", presenter and date
  SLIDE 2: WHO WE ARE — Mission statement, 3 core values with icons, company snapshot (founded, team size, HQ)
  SLIDE 3: THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE — Industry challenge, [COMPANY NAME]'s answer, key differentiator
  SLIDE 4: OUR SERVICES/PRODUCTS — 3-4 service tiles with icon, name, and 1-sentence description
  SLIDE 5: HOW WE WORK — Process flow (4-5 steps), timeline per phase, what client does vs what we do
  SLIDE 6: CASE STUDIES — 2 mini case studies: Client type | Challenge | Solution | Result (metric)
  SLIDE 7: CLIENTS & PARTNERS — Logo grid placeholder (8-12 logos), testimonial quote
  SLIDE 8: THE TEAM — Grid of team member tiles: photo placeholder, name, role, 1 key credential
  SLIDE 9: PRICING / ENGAGEMENT — Package options or "Contact us for custom pricing" with what's included
  SLIDE 10: NEXT STEPS & CONTACT — How to get started (3 steps), contact info, social handles, website

  Generate full placeholder content tailored to a [INDUSTRY] company.
usage: |
  Perfect for sales teams, agency founders, and consultants presenting to potential clients.
  Ask Claude to customize tone — "make it more casual" or "more enterprise-appropriate."
variables: |
  [COMPANY NAME] → Your company
  [INDUSTRY] → Your sector: "digital marketing agency", "SaaS company", "architecture firm"
  [YEAR] → Founded year | [COMPANY SIZE] → "5-person team" / "50 employees" / "startup"
  [TAGLINE] → Your company tagline (under 8 words)
tips: |
  • Keep to 10 slides — prospects' attention is limited in sales calls
  • Ask Claude to "write 3 version of the tagline" and pick the strongest
  • Add "include a FAQ slide addressing top 3 objections" at the end
